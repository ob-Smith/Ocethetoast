360° Magnetic Robot

Jan 2023 - Apr 2023 • University Innovation and Entrepreneurship Project (in partnership with Naval Group)

Prototype 360° inclination capable robot for weld-shaving on navy hulls

Once positioned it can be controlled via smartphone. Speeds are configurable and range extends beyond 100m. While a lot of progress was made, the project was passed onto following-year students due to a lack of time.

  • DOGA geared motors delivering ~20 Nm torque per wheel
  • 3-axis SMA (Support Multi-Axes) grinding platform
  • Grinding tool mounted in plug-and-play removable system
  • Magnetic adhesion system with ~120 kg holding force magnets

MSc Dissertation — Compliant Spatial Parallel Manipulator

May 2024 - Aug 2024 • MSc Dissertation

MSc Robotics dissertation on the development of a compliant 3RRRR spatial parallel manipulator using flexure-based CORE joints, FEA-driven design, and experimental validation.



Overview

This dissertation investigated how compliance could be integrated into an existing 3RRRR spatial parallel manipulator by replacing traditional revolute joints with flexure-based joints. The work focused on implementing the Compliant Rolling Contact Element (CORE) into the manipulator, optimizing the flexure geometry through theory and finite element analysis, and validating the final concept through a 3D-printed prototype and load testing. The study combined CAD design, stress analysis, inverse kinematics, and experimental evaluation to assess stiffness, torque resistance, workspace, and load-dependent behavior.

Key features / highlights

  • Novel compliant 3RRRR spatial parallel manipulator
  • CORE flexure joints with bending range above 180°
  • 3-limb parallel manipulator architecture
  • Approx. 184 mm platform equilibrium height
  • 3D-printed PLA prototype with guided rail system
  • FEA- and theory-driven flexure optimization

Tech & tools

  • Autodesk Inventor
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • MATLAB
  • Inverse kinematics modelling
  • PLA 3D printing
  • Bambu Lab P1P
  • Bernoulli-Euler beam theory
  • Stress and stiffness analysis
  • Experimental torque validation
  • MG996R servo motor testing

My role

  • Designed the compliant joint and manipulator CAD architecture
  • Integrated CORE flexure joints into an existing 3RRRR parallel manipulator concept
  • Performed finite element analysis to evaluate stress distribution across candidate materials
  • Derived stiffness and torque relationships using analytical modelling
  • Implemented inverse kinematics and workspace analysis in MATLAB
  • Built and experimentally tested a 3D-printed prototype under load conditions
